Build a clean, minimalist personal consulting website for a college student pursuing strategy consulting roles. The website should feel professional, structured, and similar in style to a top consulting firm (like McKinsey or BCG), but still personal.

DESIGN STYLE:
- Minimalist, lots of white space
- Black, white, and subtle gray color palette
- Clean modern font (like Inter or Helvetica)
- Smooth scrolling and subtle animations
- Mobile responsive
- Fast loading

OVERALL GOAL:
The site should immediately communicate:
“This person is structured, strategic, and already thinks like a consultant.”

NAVIGATION BAR:
- Home
- About
- Experience
- Case Studies
- Projects
- Contact

HOME PAGE:
- Strong headline:
  “Strategic Thinker. Operator. Aspiring Consultant.”
- Subheadline:
  “I break down complex problems, build structured solutions, and execute with discipline.”
- Short intro paragraph:
  A BYU Strategic Management student with experience in strategy, sales leadership, and research.
- Section: “What I Do”
  - Strategy & Problem Solving
  - Leadership & Execution
  - Data & Analysis
- Section: Featured Experience (3 highlights)
- Section: Featured Case Study (preview)
- Call to action: “View My Work” and “Get in Touch”

ABOUT PAGE:
- Story-driven narrative:
  - Background (BYU, interest in strategy)
  - Mission experience → leadership + people skills
  - Transition into business, consulting, and strategy
- Emphasize:
  - Leadership
  - Ability to connect with people
  - Breaking down messy problems

EXPERIENCE PAGE:
Create clean sections for each role with bullet points.

Include:
Business Strategy Intern
- Built and launched website and digital infrastructure
- Organized project workflows and supported growth strategy execution

Field Strategy & Sales Lead
- Generated $80K personal revenue in 4 months
- Contributed to $140K team revenue
- Recruited, trained, and led a small sales team
- Conducted performance reviews and strategy planning

Undergraduate Research Assistant
- Analyzed data and contributed to research-backed strategy insights

Mission Leadership Role
- Led and mentored 80+ individuals
- Conducted trainings and supported performance improvement

CASE STUDIES PAGE:
Create 2–3 sample case studies (structured like consulting work)

Each case study should include:
- Problem
- Approach
- Key Insights
- Recommendation

Example topics:
1. Growth strategy for a pest control company
